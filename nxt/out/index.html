Art is what makes us human. In a world of ever increasing machine use and perfection, it’s easy to feel out of place. We make mistakes. We aren’t programmed to be perfect.

My travels have taught me that the world is bursting with colour and possibilities in all different forms and my art is my way to find joy and connection in the world, to remember that it’s okay to be human and to express my true emotions. I hope that it can inspire others to find the beauty and possibility in their own lives and to follow their own dreams with colour and verve.
